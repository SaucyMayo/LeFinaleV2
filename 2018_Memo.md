## Question 1: Multiple Choice - 5 marks

**(a) Intel instruction with no stack pointer effect** [1]
**Answer: B - cmp** - Compares via subtraction but only sets flags, doesn't modify ESP.

**(b) Cannot encode in Mic-1 microcode** [1]  
**Answer: B - H = MDR - H** - H must be subtrahend, not minuend in Mic-1 ALU.

**(c) Proportional allocation: 62 frames, 10 & 127 pages** [1]
**Answer: C - 57** - Calculation: (127/137) × 62 ≈ 57 frames

**(d) Dependency type: R1=R2\*R3; R4=R5+R1** [1]
**Answer: A - RAW** - Read After Write (true dependency)

**(e) Second-chance page class evicted first** [1]
**Answer: A - (0, 0)** - Not referenced, not modified = best candidate

## Question 2: ALU Control Signals - 4 marks

| Function | F0 | F1 | ena | inva | inc |
|----------|----|----|-----|------|-----|
| A+B      | 1  | 1  | 1   | 0    | 0   |
| A+B+1    | 1  | 1  | 1   | 0    | 1   |
| B-A      | 1  | 1  | 1   | 1    | 1   |
| -A       | 1  | 1  | 0   | 1    | 1   |

**Key:** F0/F1 select function, ena enables A, inva inverts A, inc adds 1.
